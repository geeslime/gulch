Definitions:

- Desire: a feeling of pleasure in anticipation of something
- Will: the aim which is sought through purposeful behavior
- Consent: the communication of one's will to someone instrumental in attaining it
- Rape: unwilling sex

Insights:

- Consensual interaction is a proper subset of willing interaction
- Consent implies willingness, but willingness does not imply consent
- Desire has no effect on the result
