Rationalism is the [[note/Epistemology\|epistemic]] position that [[Knowledge\|knowledge]] can be derived through the use of "pure" [[Reason\|reason]] completely detached from any [[note/Sense Perception\|sensory evidence]]. This makes rationalism a form of [[note/Mysticism\|mysticism]], placing it in opposition to [[note/Empiricism\|empiricism]].
