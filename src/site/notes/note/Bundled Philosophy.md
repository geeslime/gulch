A bundled philosophy is any philosophy that gives you [[note/Metaphysics\|metaphysics]] (What is there?), [[note/Epistemology\|epistemology]] (How do I know?), and [[Ethics\|ethics]] (What should I do about it?) all in one neat bundle with the same answer to everything. [[Religion\|Religion]] is a common example: *What is there?*---God is there; *How do I know?*---[[note/Mystics of Spirit\|mystic]] contact with God; *What should I do about it?*---follow God's plan.
