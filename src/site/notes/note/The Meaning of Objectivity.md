There are two main senses of the term "objective," [[note/The Metaphysical Sense of Objectivity\|the metaphysical]], and [[note/The Epistemic Sense of Objectivity\|the epistemic]]. In general to be objective means to adhere to [[note/The Primacy of Existence vs The Primacy of Consciousness\|the primacy of existence]], whereas to be [[Subjective\|subjective]] means to adhere to the primacy of consciousness.
