"Validation" is a broader concept than "[[note/Proof\|proof]]" that refers to any process of establishing an idea's relationship to [[note/Existence Exists\|reality]], whether it be [[Deduction\|deduction]], [[Induction\|induction]], or perceptual self-evidence. [[note/Axioms as Irreducible Primaries\|Axioms]] fall under this third category: they are validated by direct sense perception, and [[note/Axioms as Undeniable\|they cannot be coherently denied]].
