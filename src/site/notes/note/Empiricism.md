Empiricism is the [[note/Epistemology\|epistemic]] view that all [[Knowledge\|knowledge]] is based on the evidence of [[note/Sense Perception\|the senses]]. In modern philosophy, [[note/The Conflation of Empiricism and Nominalism-Sensualism\|empiricism has been almost universally conflated with nominalism and sensualism]].
