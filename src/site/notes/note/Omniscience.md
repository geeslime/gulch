Omniscence means [[note/Infinity as a Floating Abstraction\|infinite]] [[Knowledge\|knowledge]]---knowledge of all things. Either the omniscient being is said to have this knowledge [[note/Innate Ideas\|innately]], or it somehow acquired all-knowledge with it's [[Reason\|reason]]. But, [[note/Innate Knowledge as a Violation of Causality\|innate ideas violate the law of cause and effect]], and [[note/The Impossibility of Gaining Omniscence\|it is impossible to gain infinite knowledge]].
