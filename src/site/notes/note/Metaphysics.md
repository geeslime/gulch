Metaphysics is the branch of philosophy that studies the nature of the universe as a whole and contains within its field of study [[note/The Fundamental Axioms of Metaphysics\|the most fundamental knowledge possible]]. In the words of [[people/Leonard Peikoff\|Leonard Peikoff]]:

>\[...] the essence of metaphysics, according to [[note/Objectivism\|Objectivism]], is the step-by-step development of the [[note/Corollary\|corollaries]] of the [[note/Existence Exists\|existence axiom]].[^1]

[^1]: [[notes/OPAR\|notes/OPAR]]OPAR]], p. 16
