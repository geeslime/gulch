- [[note/Primacy of the Supernatural Consciousness\|Primacy of the Supernatural Consciousness]]---the divine mind has primacy;
- [[note/Primacy of the Social Consciousness\|Primacy of the Social Consciousness]]---the agreement of society has primacy;
- [[note/Primacy of the Personal Consciousness\|Primacy of the Personal Consciousness]]---the thoughts of the individual has primacy.
