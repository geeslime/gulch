Sensualism is the [[note/Epistemology\|epistemic]] position which holds that the only type of cognitive event is [[note/Sense Perception\|sense perception]]---i.e. every thought, feeling, emotion, cognition, is at most reverberations--or "images"--of sense data. [[note/Nominalism iff Sensualism\|Sensualism is the epistemic component of Nominalism-Sensualism]].
