In [[note/Metaphysics\|metaphysics]], [[note/The Meaning of Objectivity\|objective]] means independent of [[note/The Axiom of Consciousness\|consciousness]]---a fact is *objective* if it is what it is independent of consciousness. The objective is that which cannot be willed away.
