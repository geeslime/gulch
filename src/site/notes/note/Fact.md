A **fact** is that which pertains to existence—something that is the case in reality as it is [[note/The Primacy of Existence vs The Primacy of Consciousness\|independent of consciousness]].[^1] [[note/Existence Exists\|Existence]], being the most fundamental concept, [[vv/existence/Clarifying ''Existence Exists''\|cannot be defined in terms of anything more basic]] than "*that which is*", and so **facts**, as the "*that which*" of existence, derive their meaning from their role as [[note/Broad Physical Categories (BPC)\|distinct manifestations of reality]]. A fact exists *independently* of any beings trying to get the facts right, i.e. any beings using facts. As such, facts can be [[G1/Conceptual File Folder\|understood and situated within our knowledge]], but they cannot be defined further. [[people/Ayn Rand\|Ayn Rand]] states, “*[[note/Truth\|Truth]] is the product of the recognition (i.e., identification) of the facts of reality.*" This means that to grasp a fact is to recognize a portion of reality as it is, without the [[note/Evasion\|deliberate disintegrating of facts]]. To hold something as true is to correctly—[[The Law of Non Contradiction\|without contradiction]]—identify and integrate a fact of reality.

Facts precede [[note/Truth\|truth]]; truth is our grasp of facts, and [[note/Falsehood\|falsity]] lies [[note/Epistemology\|in the realm of cognition]], not [[note/Metaphysics\|in reality itself]]. All truths must be comprised of facts, but truth is *not* intrinsic in any fact of reality; facts are out there in reality, truth is the [[note/Proposition\|conceptual identification]] of those facts. As [[people/Aristotle\|Aristotle]] noted, "*Falsity and truth are not in things... but in [[thought\|thought]]*" (Metaphysics 1027b25-26). Once we ascertain a fact's existence, [[note/The Fundamental Axioms of Metaphysics\|the fundamentals of Objectivist metaphysics]] is conditioned: a fact exists *independently* of consciousness(existence exists), it is an existent which one is conscious of(consciousness exists), and is what it is (the law of identity).

[[note/Objectivism\|Objectivism]] differentiates between [[note/Metaphysically Given\|metaphysical facts]], which are inherent in existence, and [[Man-Made Facts\|man-made facts]], which are [[note/Causal Connections (BPC)\|caused]] by [[note/Human Action\|human actions]] and [[note/The Locus of Human Free Will\|choices]]. Metaphysical facts, such as the laws of physics, are [[note/The Metaphysically Given as Absolute\|immutable and absolute]]. These facts exist *independently* of human action and cannot be altered by [[note/Consciousness\|consciousness]]. On the other hand, man-made facts result from human actions and choices, such as [[note/Skyscrapers as the True Dimensions of Man\|the construction of skyscrapers]] or [[Factors of Production\|the goods produced in an economy]]. Such **facts** are products of human decisions and [[note/Free Will\|can be changed by altering those decisions]]. While reality itself is absolute, the creations of human beings are *contingent upon human decisions*. However, all facts *persist even when we don't see them in front of us* and [[note/The Law of Causality\|they do what they do because of their state of affair both within themselves and between other things in the world]].

While facts are fundamental and absolute, [[note/The Epistemic Sense of Objectivity\|our understanding of them]] is always subject to the [[G1/Context\|context]] and scope of our [[G1/Knowledge\|knowledge]]. Objectivism rejects the notion of [[note/Non-Existence\|out-of-context absolutes]], a fact is [[note/Validation\|validated]] within the context of one's knowledge available at a given time. As knowledge grows, previously known facts are *not* contradicted but are seen in a [[note/Abstraction\|broader]], more [[note/Integration\|integrated]] context. Like the discovery of additional stable molecular forms of carbon, these new discoveries do not invalidate the fact that graphite and diamond are stable forms of carbon; it simply expands the contextual understanding of carbon's [[note/Entity and Property (BPC)\|properties]]. [[note/Definition\|Definitions]] are contextual and evolve as knowledge expands. A proper definition [[note/Measurement Relationships\|condenses]] a vast body of observations into a concise statement, [[note/The Role of Definition for a Conceptual Being\|capturing the essence of a concept]]. The definition of "gold" has evolved from its observable properties to include its atomic number [[note/Science\|as science advanced]]. While definitions may change, they remain grounded in the [[note/The Meaning of Objectivity\|objective]] facts of reality, reflecting a deeper and more precise understanding over time.

Ignoring or [[note/Evasion\|evading]] facts [[note/The Psychological Effects of Regarding the Metaphysically Given as Mutable\|leads to failure and destruction]], as actions based on false premises cannot achieve the intended attainment of ends. [[Value\|Values]] are not derived [[note/Forms of the Primacy of Consciousness\|from some mystic authority or whim]] but from the nature of reality and [[Life as the Standard of Value\|the requirements of human life]]. Every proper [[Value-Judegment\|value-judgment]] is an identification of a fact: something is good because it *affirms* your life, or bad because it *threatens* it. Thus, values are a type of fact, specifically *facts in relation to the choice to live*. [[Value vs Fact\|Every fact entails an evaluation because it has implications for human action and survival]]. 

[^1]: [[note/Consciousness is an Active Process\|Consciousness is epistemologically active but metaphysically it is passive]].
