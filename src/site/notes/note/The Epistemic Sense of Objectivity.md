In the [[note/Epistemology\|epistemic]] sense to be [[note/The Meaning of Objectivity\|objective]] means to adhere to the object--the object of cognition, namely reality--in coming to conclusions. In other words, objectivity means being guided by existence in a given thought process as against the arbitrary content of ones [[note/The Axiom of Consciousness\|consciousness]], which would be [[Subjective\|subjective]].
