**Psuedoscience** is the practice of systematic [[note/Evasion\|evasion]] within a specialized [[note/Science\|science]]. Proponents, don't disagree with the fundamental questions within the relevant field, however, they will appeal to [[note/Fallacy of Reification of the Zero\|a primacy of doubt]] to evade the body of [[Knowledge\|knowledge]], [[Context\|context]], or [[note/Validation\|established]] [[note/Evidence\|evidence]] within their field. Instead of expanding upon or delimiting the context of [[note/Proof\|proven]] discoveries, or [[Reduction\|reducing]] the field's [[Fundamentality\|fundamental]] question from the [[note/Concept\|ideas]] it [[Induction\|subsumes]], or [[note/The Law of Identity\|refining the field's question and methods]], they seek to inquire about [[Fact\|facts of reality]] from outside of [[note/Existence Exists\|reality]] i.e. on an unfounded, [[note/Arbitrary\|arbitrary]], [[note/Non-Existence\|non-existent]] basis.


Under this definition. [[Galileo\|Galileo]] was not a pseudoscientist, as he had strong evidence for his claim and was persecuted by those who held to their preffered [[note/The Primacy of Existence vs The Primacy of Consciousness\|primacy of consciousness]]. The consistent application of the primacy of existence is the necessary prerequisite for any study of any field to remain scientific as opposed to being a ***" science of evasion."*** One cannot apply [[Cartesian Doubt\|cartesian doubt]] to everything within a field to make their [[Negative Propositions\|arbitrary claims]] and still be considered *a thinker—of any field of thought, not a seeker of its knowledge, or a true lover of wisdom of any sort.*
